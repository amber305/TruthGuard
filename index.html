How It Works

Our advanced system uses multiple techniques to verify news authenticity

1

Content Analysis

Our system analyzes the text for sensationalist language, clickbait titles, and emotional manipulation tactics.

2

Source Verification

We check the credibility of the source against our database of known reliable and unreliable publishers.

3

Cross-Reference

The content is cross-referenced with verified facts and other reporting on the same topic.

4

AI Evaluation

Our machine learning algorithms evaluate patterns and provide a confidence score on the content's authenticity.
